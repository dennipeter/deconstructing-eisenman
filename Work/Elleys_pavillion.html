    Elleys Pavillion

    Visual / Interactive
    Collaborators
    Ocupus Studio
    Chopra Designs
    Photographs / Video
    Siddhesh Dave
    We built an interactive lobby for Elleys at their exhibition pavilion during the ACE Tech Expo 2022. Guests are greeted with a 10 x 3m LED wall which wraps around the entire lobby. A depth-tracking camera captures the movement of guests within the space and projects it back onto the LED wall, creating an interactive experience for the users. The visuals are based on the design of the latest switches by the "Elleys" brand.

    In its natural state, the animation on the screen is slow and fluid. But when the camera detects a user within the space, the patterns of the visuals immediately change and get much more active, thus drawing the guest's attention towards the screens. The user's silhouette within a particular region is tracked and mapped back onto the LED screens close to their physical location.
